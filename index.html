Hello, I’m Ian.

Background

I’m an aspiring web designer who loves everything about the web. Ever since I was eight, I have had a passion for the web and design. I still remember getting my first giant HTML book and feverishly reading through it. I know my passion will allow me to bring to life ideas and designs for the web.

Design has been my passion for many years now, and transitioning from a 3D focus in games to the web is something I am excited to share with the world.

    • I am also a huge fan of pixel art.
